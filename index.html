HOW IT WORKS

How it works.

Commitments that are made, tracked, and honored — for your relationships and your goals.

VOL. 01

Promise to Others.

Two-way commitments with the people who matter. Both sides confirm completion. No more empty "let's hang soon."

VOL. 02

Promise to Self.

Personal goals only you can see — or share with the world. Your word matters even when it's whispered.

VOL. 03

Promise to the World.

Commit to something bigger. Donate, volunteer, create. Finish one, and we challenge you to go further.

VOL. 04

Track & Grow.

Live feed, countdown timers, and a reliability score that reflects who you are at your word. Not a game — a mirror.
